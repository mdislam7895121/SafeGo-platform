## 1. PRIVACY POLICY

### Purpose
This policy explains how SafeGo collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information from customers, drivers, and partners.

### Scope
Applies to all users of SafeGo services in Bangladesh and the United States.

### Information We Collect

**From Customers:**
- Name, phone number, email address
- Location data during active rides
- Trip history and preferences
- Device information (IP address, app version)

**From Drivers:**
- Full name and contact details
- Government-issued identification
  - Bangladesh: National ID (NID)
  - United States: Driver's license, SSN (last 4 digits)
- Vehicle registration details
- Bank/mobile money account for payouts
- Real-time location during active duty
- Earnings and trip records

**From Partners (Restaurants, Shops):**
- Business name and trade license
- Owner contact information
- Bank account for settlements
- Order history and transaction records

### How We Use Information
- To provide and improve services
- To verify identity and prevent fraud
- To process payments and settlements
- To communicate about trips, orders, and updates
- To comply with legal requirements

### Data Sharing
We share data only with:
- Customers and drivers (for active trips)
- Payment processors (for transactions)
- Government authorities (when legally required)
- Service providers (with data protection agreements)

### Data Protection
- All sensitive data is encrypted
- Access is restricted to authorized personnel
- Regular security audits are conducted

### Your Rights
- Request access to your data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion (subject to legal retention)
- Withdraw consent for non-essential processing

### Country-Specific Rules

**Bangladesh:**
- NID is stored securely and used only for verification
- Cash transaction records are maintained for settlement purposes
- Data may be shared with Bangladesh Bank if required

**United States:**
- SSN (last 4 digits) used for background verification only
- Data handling complies with state privacy laws
- Right to opt-out of data sale (we do not sell data)

### Enforcement
Violations of data protection requirements may result in account suspension and legal action.

### Pilot Note
During pilot phase, data collection is limited to essential operational needs only.

## 3. DRIVER TERMS & AGREEMENT

### Purpose
This agreement governs the relationship between SafeGo and independent driver-partners who provide transportation services.

### Scope
Applies to all drivers registered on SafeGo in Bangladesh and the United States.

### Driver Status
- Drivers are independent contractors, not employees
- SafeGo provides the technology platform only
- Drivers choose when and where to work
- No minimum hours required

### Key Rules

**Eligibility:**
- Valid driving license for vehicle category
- Minimum age: 21 years (BD), 21 years (US)
- Clean background (no serious criminal record)
- Vehicle must meet platform standards

**Required Documents:**

*Bangladesh:*
- National ID Card (NID)
- Valid driving license
- Vehicle registration certificate
- Tax identification (if applicable)

*United States:*
- Government-issued ID
- Valid driver's license
- Vehicle registration and insurance
- Background check clearance

**Operational Rules:**
- Accept only trips you can complete
- Follow traffic laws at all times
- Maintain vehicle in safe condition
- Dress appropriately and maintain hygiene
- No discrimination against customers
- No overcharging beyond app fare
- No soliciting rides outside the app

**Earnings & Payments:**

*Bangladesh:*
- Drivers collect cash from customers
- Platform commission (20%) is deducted weekly
- Weekly settlement every Sunday-Monday
- Negative balances must be cleared before payout

*United States:*
- Earnings deposited to linked bank account
- Weekly payout schedule
- Commission deducted automatically

**Safety Requirements:**
- Complete all safety training
- Report accidents immediately
- Maintain emergency contact updated
- Follow COVID/health guidelines when applicable

### Enforcement
Violations may result in:
- Warning with reason documented
- Temporary suspension (1-30 days)
- Permanent blocking
- Legal action for criminal activity

### Pilot Note
During pilot, driver support is limited to business hours. Settlement may be adjusted based on operational needs.

## 7. PAYMENT & CASH HANDLING POLICY

### Purpose
This policy governs how payments are collected, processed, and settled on SafeGo.

### Scope
Applies to all transactions on the platform.

### Key Rules

**Bangladesh (Pilot):**
- Primary method: Cash payment to driver
- Driver collects full fare from customer
- SafeGo commission (20%) deducted during weekly settlement
- Settlement cycle: Sunday close, Monday-Thursday collection
- Drivers must maintain positive wallet balance after settlement
- Negative balance = must be cleared before next payout

**United States (Future):**
- Primary method: Online payment (card/digital wallet)
- Fares deducted automatically
- Driver earnings deposited weekly
- Tips go 100% to driver

**Cash Handling Rules (BD):**
- Drivers must provide change when possible
- Customers should pay exact fare when possible
- Disputes over cash payment escalated to admin
- Receipt available in-app for all trips

**Settlement Process (BD):**
1. Week ends Sunday midnight
2. Monday: Statement generated
3. Tuesday-Thursday: Cash collection at hub / mobile transfer
4. Friday: Enforcement for unpaid balances

### Enforcement
- Late settlement: Warning, then suspension
- Cash discrepancies: Investigation, potential blocking
- Fraud: Immediate blocking and legal action

### Pilot Note
During pilot, only cash payments are accepted in Bangladesh. Online payment integration planned for future.

## 8. REFUND & CANCELLATION POLICY

### Purpose
This policy explains when and how refunds and cancellations are processed.

### Scope
Applies to all SafeGo services.

### Cancellation Rules

**By Customer:**
| Timing | Fee |
|--------|-----|
| Within 2 minutes of booking | Free |
| After driver assigned, before arrival | ৳20 BD / $2 US |
| After driver arrives (no-show) | ৳50 BD / $5 US |

**By Driver:**
- Driver cancellation allowed for safety reasons
- Excessive cancellations result in warning/suspension
- Customer receives full refund for driver cancellation

### Refund Eligibility

**Eligible:**
- Driver did not show up
- Trip not completed as booked
- Overcharge verified by admin
- Service quality issue confirmed

**Not Eligible:**
- Customer changed mind after trip started
- Minor delays due to traffic
- Disputes without evidence

### Refund Process
1. Submit refund request in-app within 48 hours
2. Admin reviews within 3 business days
3. Approved refunds processed to wallet or original payment method

### Enforcement
- False refund claims result in account warning
- Repeated abuse leads to suspension

### Pilot Note
During pilot, refund processing may take up to 5 business days.

## 9. DRIVER BLOCKING & SUSPENSION POLICY

### Purpose
This policy explains when and how drivers may be blocked or suspended from SafeGo.

### Scope
Applies to all registered drivers.

### Suspension Levels

| Level | Duration | Trigger |
|-------|----------|---------|
| Warning | N/A | First minor violation |
| Temporary Suspension | 1-7 days | Repeated violations, customer complaints |
| Extended Suspension | 8-30 days | Serious policy breach |
| Permanent Block | Indefinite | Criminal activity, fraud, safety threat |

### Reasons for Action

**Minor Violations (Warning):**
- Late to pickup (occasional)
- Incomplete trip without valid reason
- Customer rating below threshold

**Serious Violations (Suspension):**
- Overcharging customers
- Rude or aggressive behavior
- Cancellation abuse
- Failure to settle commission

**Critical Violations (Permanent Block):**
- Criminal activity
- Assault or harassment
- Fraud or fake documents
- Repeated serious violations

### Process
1. Admin documents the reason (required)
2. Driver notified via app/SMS
3. Block takes effect immediately
4. Appeal may be submitted within 7 days

### Appeal Process
- Submit appeal through support
- Provide evidence if applicable
- Decision within 5 business days
- Admin decision is final

### Country-Specific Rules

**Bangladesh:**
- Outstanding commission must be paid even after blocking
- NID flagged for future applications

**United States:**
- Background check failure = automatic ineligibility
- Legal action for criminal violations

### Pilot Note
During pilot, all blocking decisions are reviewed by platform administrator.

## 12. DISPUTE RESOLUTION POLICY

### Purpose
This policy explains how disputes between users, drivers, and SafeGo are resolved.

### Scope
Applies to all disputes arising from platform use.

### Dispute Categories

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Fare Disputes | Wrong charge, overcharge, missing refund |
| Service Quality | Rude driver, unsafe driving, wrong route |
| Payment Issues | Failed payment, double charge |
| Property Damage | Item left in vehicle, vehicle damage |
| Safety Concerns | Harassment, assault, accident |

### Resolution Process

**Step 1: In-App Report**
- Submit dispute within 48 hours
- Provide trip ID and description
- Upload evidence if available

**Step 2: Admin Review**
- Case assigned within 24 hours

- Evidence reviewed

**Step 3: Resolution**
- Decision within 3-5 business days
- Actions: Refund, warning, suspension, or no action
- Both parties notified

**Step 4: Appeal (if applicable)**
- Appeal within 7 days of decision
- Senior admin reviews
- Final decision within 5 business days

### Escalation
- Unresolved disputes may be escalated to local authorities
- SafeGo cooperates with legal investigations

### Country-Specific Rules

**Bangladesh:**
- Major disputes may involve local arbitration
- Consumer complaints handled per DNCRP guidelines

**United States:**
- Arbitration clause applies (see Terms of Service)
- Small claims court available for disputes under $5,000

## 13. FRAUD & ABUSE POLICY

### Purpose
This policy defines fraud and abuse, and explains consequences.

### Scope
Applies to all platform users.

### Types of Fraud

**Customer Fraud:**
- Fake refund claims
- Multiple accounts for promotions
- Payment fraud (fake cards, chargebacks)

**Driver Fraud:**
- Fake trips (GPS manipulation)
- Overcharging customers outside app
- Fake documents during registration
- Cash collection without reporting

**Partner Fraud:**
- Fake orders for commission
- Misrepresenting products/services
- Tax evasion using platform data

### Detection Methods
- Automated pattern detection
- Customer/driver reports
- Settlement discrepancy analysis
- GPS and device fingerprinting

### Consequences

| Severity | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Minor (first offense) | Warning + monitoring |
| Moderate | Suspension + recovery of losses |
| Severe | Permanent ban + legal action |

### Reporting Fraud
- Use in-app report feature
- Email: fraud@safegoglobal.com (future)
- Confidential reporting available

### Country-Specific Rules

**Bangladesh:**
- Cash fraud reported to local authorities
- COD fraud prevention measures active

**United States:**
- Payment fraud reported to card networks
- Identity theft reported to FTC

### Pilot Note
All fraud cases during pilot reviewed by platform administrator.

## 14. SAFETY & EMERGENCY POLICY

### Purpose
This policy outlines safety measures and emergency procedures.

### Scope
Applies to all users during active trips/orders.

### Safety Features

**For Customers:**
- Real-time trip tracking
- Share trip with trusted contacts
- Emergency SOS button
- Driver information visible

**For Drivers:**
- Customer information for contact
- Emergency SOS button
- Report unsafe customer
- GPS tracking for protection

### Emergency Procedures

**During Active Trip:**
1. Press SOS button in app (if available)
2. Call local emergency services
   - Bangladesh: 999
   - United States: 911
3. Contact SafeGo support
4. Stay in safe location

**After Incident:**
1. Report in-app immediately
2. Provide incident details
3. Upload photos/evidence if safe
4. Follow up with support

### Accident Protocol
1. Ensure safety of all parties
2. Call emergency services if needed
3. Document with photos
4. Report to SafeGo within 24 hours
5. Cooperate with investigation

### Prohibited Safety Behaviors
- Driving under influence
- Using phone while driving (except navigation)
- Carrying weapons in vehicle
- Threatening or intimidating behavior

### Enforcement
- Safety violations = immediate suspension
- Criminal behavior = permanent ban + police report

### Pilot Note
SOS feature may have limited functionality during pilot. Use local emergency numbers directly if needed.
